# LA City Data Visualization Analysis

## Overview
Python-based analysis of Los Angeles city business data, exploring business registrations and openings across council districts using data visualization techniques.

## Project Description
Developed comprehensive visualizations analyzing LA city business data from a JSON dataset. Created multiple chart types to identify patterns in business distribution and growth across different council districts.

## Visualizations

### Scatter Plot: Business Openings by Council District
![Scatter Plot](visualizations/scatter-plot.png)
- Shows businesses that have opened across council districts
- Color-coded by council district
- X-axis: Start year
- Y-axis: Council district

### Histogram: Business Registration Frequency
![Histogram](visualizations/histogram.png)
- Displays the frequency of registered businesses per council district
- Identifies districts with highest business concentration

## Key Findings
- Analyzed business registration patterns across LA council districts
- Identified temporal trends in business openings
- Compared business density across different districts

## Technologies Used
- Python
- Seaborn (visualization)
- Pandas (data manipulation)
- JSON (data format)

## Files
- `analysis.py` - Main Python script with visualization code
- `visualizations/` - Output charts and plots
- `data/` - LA city business dataset (JSON format)
